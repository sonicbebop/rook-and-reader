Why homeschool families choose us

Most enrichment options nearby are one-off sessions with no throughline. Ours are built as an actual sequence — whether that's a reading list that grows with the student or a chess curriculum that ends in real tournament play.

How classes run

Small groups, banded by age and level rather than a single mixed classroom. Sessions are designed to fit a homeschool schedule and can be paid for using Florida education savings funds where eligible.
